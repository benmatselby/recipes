A Stanley Tucci classic.

## Ingredients

- Ice
- 50ml Gin (or vodka)
- 25ml sweet vermouth
- 25ml Campari
- Orange to garnish

## Method

- Add all the ice to a cocktail shaker.
- Add in the booze.
- Shake up good and proper.
- Serve straight up in a couple (You can also have it over the rocks).
- Garnish with a slice of orange.
