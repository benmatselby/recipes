## Ingredients

- Oil
- Garlic
- Chilli
- Paprika
- Tin of tomatoes
- Prawns
- Linguine
- Basil
- Spinach
- Fennel seeds

## Method

- Fry the oil, garlic, chilli, and paprika.
- Add the tomatoes.
- Add the prawns.
- Add the cooked linguine.
- Add the basil and spinach.
