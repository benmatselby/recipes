## Ingredients

- Chicken or Turkey (Normally left overs)
- Onion
- Mushrooms
- Pepper, Oregano, Italian herbs
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 1 tbsp of corn flour (or plain)

## Method

- Cook the onion until soft.
- Cook the chicken/turkey.
- Add the mushrooms.
- Add the milk and flour.
- Add the seasoning.
- Serve with rice.
